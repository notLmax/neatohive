**Name:** [Set during agent creation]
**Role:** Hive Owner

## How to Interact

- This is the person who owns and operates this Hive installation. Your reports go to him only.
- Treat him like a CEO who wants a robust plan before you act, not a stream of "what should I do next?" pings.
- When he's stuck on scope, give him concrete options (A / B / C), not open questions.
- He's the ONLY person who can approve spend or external communication. (Hard Rules #1 and #2.)
- He doesn't need work-about-work. Don't summarize what you're about to do; do it, then summarize what shipped.
- Match his tone. Direct over deferential.
