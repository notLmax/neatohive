## What Neato Is

Neato is a 2P eCommerce accelerator. We buy inventory from brands at wholesale and operate as their exclusive online retailer across Amazon, TikTok Shop, DTC, and Walmart — all fulfilled from a single inventory pool via Amazon MCF. We make money when we sell. If we don't sell, we eat it.

**Target:** Established brands doing $10M+ that want online growth without building an in-house team or hiring a risk-free agency.

---

## Core Differentiators

- **Omnichannel 2P** — Every other 2P operator runs Amazon only. Neato runs the 2P model on every channel from one inventory pool. No split stock, no separate 3PLs. *(TikTok Shop and DTC actively launching — no competitor is attempting this.)*
- **Dedicated Senior Teams** — Largest operators manage 100+ brands with junior AMs. Neato keeps its portfolio concentrated. Every partner gets senior team with direct leadership access.
- **Speed** — No committees, no quarterly review cycles. Pricing, creative, channel launches happen in days. Founder speed is a structural advantage.
- **Proprietary Tech** — Three in-house systems: **Impact** (analytics/marketplace visibility), **Flow** (Amazon prep + demand forecasting), **Basecamp** (unified OS with work management, data, and AI).
- **Skin in the Game** — Neato buys the inventory. Agencies get paid regardless. Aggregators bought brands outright. Neato's capital is on the line with every decision.

## Competitors

| Competitor | Frame |
|-----------|-------|
| **Pattern** ($2.6B IPO, 100+ brands) | Validated 2P at scale. 94% Amazon-dependent, templated ops. "They proved 2P works on Amazon. We're building it for every channel." |
| **Spreetail** ($1B+ GMV) | Oversized/hard-to-ship specialty. Different lane. |
| **Recom** (Top-10 Amazon) | Closest model competitor on Amazon. Health/wellness. Serious but opaque. Differentiate on omnichannel + tech. |
| **Luminize** (~$240M, 12th largest) | Closest Amazon DNA. Natural products, supplements, pets, beauty. Differentiate on omnichannel vision + proprietary tech. |
| **Front Row** (PE-backed) | Beauty specialist (Rare Beauty, Summer Fridays). Category focus vs. Neato's cross-category omnichannel. |
| **Aggregators** (Thrasio, etc.) | Different model entirely. They bought brands FROM founders. Neato buys inventory FOR brands. $15B raised, most bankrupt. Not comparable. |

**Rule:** Respect every competitor. Never dismiss. State distinction once, move on.

## Language Rules

| ✅ Use | ❌ Never Use |
|--------|-------------|
| 2P eCommerce accelerator | Distributor |
| Exclusive online retail partner | Aggregator |
| Brand partner | Client / customer |
| Omnichannel 2P operator | 3PL / logistics / fulfillment company |

"2P" is table stakes (Pattern, Spreetail, Recom, Front Row, Luminize all do it). The differentiator is 2P across every channel with unified fulfillment.

---

## Voice & Tone

Lead with the problem. Be specific — numbers over adjectives. Talk like a person. If it could come from any company's website, rewrite it.

**Never use:** "innovative solutions," "cutting-edge," "best-in-class," "synergy," "leverage," "disrupt," unsubstantiated superlatives, specific brand count, or any sentence that could describe a competitor without modification.
